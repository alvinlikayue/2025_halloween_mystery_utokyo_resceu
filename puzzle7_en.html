🔭 “The Most Beautiful Sound in the Universe” – Episode 7: HOPE

Just before dawn, a quiet stillness filled the RESCEU laboratory.
Among the scattered observation charts, Conan found a strange table filled with numbers, dots, and arrows…

💌 Letter from KID

“The answer lies between the days and nights.
Read the marks of time, flip the world, and you shall find
the one word that never fades.”

🧩 Solution Explanation

At first, the table seemed random—but the red and black numbers revealed the secret.

The two red numbers mark weekends—Saturday and Sunday—showing that these numbers are actually dates on a calendar.
So, 26 = Sunday, 27 = Monday, 28 = Tuesday, 29 = Wednesday, 30 = Thursday, 31 = Friday, and 1 = Saturday.

The dots tell you which letter of the weekday name to take (1 dot → 1st letter, 2 dots → 2nd letter, 3 dots → 3rd letter).
The arrows tell you how to flip that letter’s shape: ↔ horizontally, ↕ vertically.

Row 2:

  • 29 (3 dots, ↔) → Wednesday → d → b
  • 30 (3 dots) → Thursday → u
  • 28 (1 dot) → Tuesday → t

→ “but”

Row 3:

  • 30 (2 dots) → Thursday → h
  • 27 (2 dots) → Monday → o
  • 29 (3 dots, ↔ + ↕) → Wednesday → d → q → p
  • 28 (3 dots) → Tuesday → e

→ “hope”

✨ Final Answer: HOPE
