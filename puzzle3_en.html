🌟 The Kindest Sound in the Universe — Episode 3: Labyrinth of Light

The morning after solving the previous night’s puzzle,
another white dove appeared before Conan and his friends.

Tied to its leg was a silver envelope, reflecting the sunlight like a beam of light.

Inside, a single sentence was written in shining ink:
“The next treasure lies at the end of the Path of Light.”

Haibara

It feels like… a maze.

Conan

No — it’s a labyrinth of light. It shows the optical paths running through this facility.

Agasa

Hmm… it looks like the beams are reflecting off mirrors, but where are they headed!?

Mitsuhiko

The arrows point in all directions! I don’t even see an exit!

Conan

Which means… this puzzle can only be solved if you understand the rules of light.

Conan

Inside this facility, light changes direction when it hits mirrors or beam splitters.
A beam splitter both reflects and transmits light — half and half.

🌟 Summary: Optical Components and Light Behavior

  • 🟥 Light Path (Red Line)
    Shows the path of light. It follows the direction of the arrows.
    When it meets mirrors or beam splitters, its route changes.
  • 🪞 Mirror
    Reflects light according to the law of reflection: angle of incidence = angle of reflection.
  • 🔷 Beam Splitter
    A half-transparent mirror that splits light into two paths — one transmitted, one reflected.
  • ⬛ Black Square (Stop Point)
    Light stops upon hitting it. No reflection.
Haibara

So if we follow the light correctly… the answer will appear.

Conan

Exactly — the word revealed by the “true path of light” is Kid’s target.

Conan

Following the red beam... letters start to appear... L → A → S → E → R!

Haibara

LASER…!

Agasa

Of course! A device that amplifies and interferes light to measure distances — the very heart of this observatory!

Conan

So Kid’s “Path of Light” was pointing to the LASER itself.

Reflected light gathered above — and a white silhouette appeared in the glow.

Kaito Kid

Well done, detective. You’ve escaped the labyrinth of light.

Conan

But wherever there’s light… there’s always a shadow.

Kaito Kid

Heh… then let’s meet again — where light and shadow cross, under the moonlight.
