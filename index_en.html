🧩 About the Creator

Alvin Li
Researcher at the Research Center for the Early Universe (RESCEU), The University of Tokyo.
He studies invisible signals from the cosmos — uncovering the stories and truths hidden within them.

In the summer of 2024, he completed his Ph.D. at the California Institute of Technology (Caltech),
under the supervision of Professor Alan Weinstein .
He now continues his research under Professor Kipp Cannon at the University of Tokyo.

This interactive series, “The Kindest Sound in the Universe,”
was created as a small adventure connecting the worlds of science and mystery.
Every puzzle is inspired by real science.
